SAFETY TIPS FOR KIDS COOKING

Always Have an Adult Nearby

Every recipe needs a grown-up kitchen buddy for supervision and help with tricky parts.

Wash Your Hands

Clean hands make clean food! Scrub with soap for 20 seconds before cooking.

Ask for Help

Sharp knives, hot stoves, and ovens need adult help. Never be shy to ask!

Read the Whole Recipe First

Check for any foods you're allergic to and make sure you have everything you need.

Clean As You Go

Wipe up spills right away so nobody slips, and keep your workspace tidy.

Use Oven Mitts

Hot things stay hot! Always use oven mitts when handling anything from the oven or stove.
